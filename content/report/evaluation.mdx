## 5. Evaluation

### 5.1 Lessons Learned

For the turbine design, several initial iterations were flawed due to incorrect assumptions. We simplified to a 2D approach before extending to 3D; key variables (size, rotational speed) were parameterised. Blade profiles were simplified; CAD import was resolved via DXF conversion.

### 5.2 Future Development

Structural verification of sCO₂ piping (up to 35 MPa, thermal gradients &gt; 300 °C) is essential. Proposed: strain-monitoring at HX2 tube-to-header welds, turbine casing, and high-temperature sCO₂ piping using a combined Sagnac/Michelson interferometer for high precision and stability.
